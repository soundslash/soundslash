What is Soundslash?

Soundslash is a platform for full-featured and scalable audio streaming.

We believe music should be accessible everywhere to everyone and without any effort. Soundslash is built to bring streaming platform to everyone. We designed Soundslash to be easy, that you could start your own full-featured internet radio in few seconds.

With Soundslash you can:

  • stream audio right from your browser (HTML 5, Flash),
  • manage radio on-air,
  • manage your internet radio library,
  • create playlists,
  • set up program,
  • set up advertisements or jingles,
  • create custom reports (XSL, HTML, CSV, PDF),
  • see statistics of your listeners,
  • share and play your radio on your web site,
  • scale your internet radio listeners to millions.

Try it for free!

What do I need to start internet radio?

Web browser.
